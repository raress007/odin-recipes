American Pancakes (12 portions)

Description

American-style Pancakes

Prep: 40 min | Total: 40 min | Serves: 12 pieces | Difficulty: Easy

A classic weekend breakfast made simple. These thick, fluffy pancakes come together from just seven pantry staples, butter, milk, eggs, sugar, plain flour, baking powder, and a pinch of sea salt, delivering that signature golden stack with a soft, airy centre.

Quick to mix and ready in under an hour, they're the kind of crowd-pleasing recipe that works equally well for a lazy Sunday morning or a sweet midweek treat. Serve with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whatever topping you like best.

Ingredients

  • 50g unsalted butter (plus extra for frying)
  • 300g milk
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 30g sugar
  • 200g plain flour
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder (15g)
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt

Tips

  • A popular choice when in need of a treat, these sweet, thick pancakes are whisked up in seconds with Thermomix®. For a savoury alternative, try the Ham and Cheese Crêpes on Cookidoo®.
  • Serve pancakes with maple syrup, whipped cream, chocolate, caramel sauce or jam.
  • To replace butter with oil, omit step 1 and add 50 g oil along with all other ingredients in step 2. Use oil instead of butter for frying.
  • Blueberry or Raspberry Pancakes: scatter 1 Tbsp berries onto each pancake immediately after pouring batter into pan, before bubbles start to form
  • Chocolate Pancakes: add 2 tsp cocoa powder in step 2.

Steps

  1. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/70°C/speed 1.
  2. Add milk, eggs, sugar, flour, baking powder and salt then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. To cook pancakes, heat 1/2 tsp butter in a frying pan over a medium heat. For each pancake, pour in a ladle of batter and cook for 1-2 minutes or until bubbles appear on surface. Flip pancake over and cook for a further 1 minute. Transfer pancake to a serving plate and cover to keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve hot (see tip).
