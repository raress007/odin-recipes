Brownies (24 portions)

Description

Brownies

Prep: 10 min | Total: 50 min | Serves: 24 pieces | Difficulty: Easy

Rich, dense, and deeply chocolatey, these brownies are built on a base of 70% dark chocolate and butter, sweetened with sugar and bound with six eggs for that characteristic fudgy texture. The batter comes together in just 10 minutes, with optional walnuts adding a satisfying crunch to each square.

Simple enough for a beginner but impressive enough to share, they cut into 24 generous pieces, perfect for a bake sale, an after-school treat, or whenever a serious chocolate craving strikes.

Ingredients

  • 220g butter, unsalted (cut in pieces, plus extra for greasing)
  • 250g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids (cut in pieces)
  • 380g sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 150g flour
  • 150g shelled halved walnuts (optional)
