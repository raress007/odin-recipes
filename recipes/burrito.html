Description

Tex-Mex Tortillas with Shredded Pork and Black Beans

Prep: 30 min | Total: 1 h 20 min | Serves: 6 | Difficulty: Easy

These loaded Tex-Mex tortillas bring bold, smoky flavours to the table with minimal effort. Tender boneless pork is slow-cooked with tomatoes, jalapeños, red and green capsicum, and black beans in a rich tomato-based sauce, then shredded and piled into warm corn tortillas.

Finished with creamy avocado, a dollop of sour cream, fresh coriander, and a squeeze of lime, each tortilla is a satisfying balance of heat, freshness, and comfort, ideal for a weeknight dinner or a casual spread with friends.

  • 40g olive oil
  • 600g pork (boneless, cut into cubes - 6cm x 4cm)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 150g brown onion (cut into wedges)
  • 200g ripe tomatoes (cut into pieces)
  • 20-30g pickled jalapeño chillies (or 1-2 fresh chillies, deseeded and cut into slices)
  • 120g red capsicum (deseeded and cut into pieces - 2 cm)
  • 120g green capsicum (deseeded and cut into pieces - 2 cm)
  • 250g canned black beans (250 drained water weigth)
  • 30g tomato paste
  • 100g water
  • 1/2 Chicken stock paste (see tip)
  • 2 avocados (flesh only)
  • 6 corn tortillas
  • 100g sour cream
  • 4 Tbsp coriander (leaves only)
  • 1 lime (cut into wedges)

Tips

  • For the Chicken stock paste recipe, please refer to the recipe on Cookidoo®.

Steps

  1. Place 20 g olive oil, 300 g pork, 1/2 tsp salt and 1 pinch ground black pepper into mixing bowl and start High Temp. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 20 g olive oil, remaining 300 g pork, 1/4 tsp salt and 1 pinch ground black pepper into mixing bowl and start High Temp. Transfer into thermal serving bowl with the reserved browned pork and set aside.
  3. Place 20 g of the meat juices, onion, tomatoes, chillies, capsicum into mixing bowl and start High Temp.
  4. Add reserved browned pork, 50 g of the black beans, tomato paste, water and stock paste and cook 20 min/110°C/counter clockwise/speed 1.
  5. Cook for a further 10 min/105°C//speed 1, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, then shred 4 sec/counter clockwise/speed 4
  6. Add the remaining 200 g black beans, mix gently with aid of spatula and cook 2 min/100°C/counter clockwise/speed minimal. Meanwhile, cut avocado into thin slices. Serve shredded pork and black beans with corn tortillas garnished with sour cream, avocado, coriander and lime wedges on the side.
